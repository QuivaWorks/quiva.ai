# Shared With Me Section

The **Shared With Me** section in your sidebar displays all collaborative sessions that have been shared with you by teammates. It's your central hub for team collaboration.

## Finding Shared Sessions

### Location

The **Shared With Me** section appears in your left sidebar:
```
Sidebar
├── Favorites
├── Personal Assistants
├── Team Assistants
└── Shared With Me ← You are here
├── Sarah's Sessions (3 unread)
├── Jake's Sessions (0 unread)
└── Marketing Team Sessions (5 unread)
```

### Organization

Sessions are automatically grouped by:

1. **Who shared them** – Grouped by teammate or team name
2. **Assistant type** – Further organized by which assistant
3. **Last activity** – Sorted by most recent update within each group

![Shared With Me Section](image-placeholder-shared-with-me.png)

## Session Information

Each shared session shows:

- **Session name/title** – What the session is about
- **Assistant name** – Which assistant is in use
- **Unread badge** – How many new messages (if any)
- **Last activity** – When the session was last updated
- **Participants** – How many people are in the session

## Opening a Shared Session

**Option 1: Click from sidebar**
1. Navigate to **Shared With Me**
2. Click the session you want to join
3. The full conversation loads

**Option 2: From notification**
1. Click the notification from a teammate
2. Taken directly to the new update

**Option 3: Search**
1. Use the search bar in the sidebar
2. Type the session name or teammate name
3. Click the result

## Managing Shared Sessions

### Mark as Read

Badges disappear when you:
- Open the session and view new messages
- Scroll to the bottom
- Refresh the page

### Remove Yourself from a Session

1. Click the **three-dot menu** on the session
2. Select **Remove me from session**
3. You'll no longer see it in Shared With Me

**Note:** The session owner can add you back anytime.

### Mute Notifications

To stop receiving notifications for a specific session:

1. Click the **three-dot menu** on the session
2. Select **Mute notifications**
3. You'll still see the session, but won't be notified of updates

To unmute:
1. Click the three-dot menu again
2. Select **Unmute notifications**

### Pin/Favorite a Session

Frequently accessed sessions can be pinned:

1. Click the **three-dot menu** on the session
2. Select **Add to Favorites**
3. The session moves to your **Favorites** section at the top

## Shared With Me Best Practices

### Staying Organized

- **Pin important sessions** – Keep active projects at the top
- **Mute inactive sessions** – Reduce notification noise
- **Remove old sessions** – Clean up after projects complete
- **Use search** – Find sessions by participant or assistant name

### Collaboration Tips

- **Review unread badges regularly** – Don't let updates pile up
- **Respond in shared sessions** – Keep conversations moving
- **Leave feedback** – Help teammates by contributing your thoughts
- **Archive old sessions** – Keep Shared With Me current (if supported)

### Permission Boundaries

You can:
- ✅ View all session messages
- ✅ Ask the assistant follow-up questions
- ✅ See teammate responses
- ✅ Contribute your input

You cannot:
- ❌ Remove other team members (only the owner can)
- ❌ Delete the session (only the owner can)
- ❌ Add other people (only the owner can add)

## Troubleshooting

**Q: Why don't I see a session that was shared with me?**  
A: Check your notification settings. Sessions may be filtered if notifications are disabled. You can also search for the teammate's name.

**Q: Why did a session disappear from Shared With Me?**  
A: The owner may have removed you from the session. Check with them to be added back.

**Q: Can I see who the original owner is?**  
A: Yes, look at the group name in Shared With Me. It shows who owns/shared the session.

**Q: How long does a shared session stay visible?**  
A: Indefinitely, unless the owner deletes it or removes you.
