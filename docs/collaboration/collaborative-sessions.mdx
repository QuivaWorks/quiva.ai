# Collaborative Sessions

Collaborative sessions enable you and your team to work together on AI-powered tasks in real-time. Everyone sees the same conversation thread, live updates, and results as they happen.

## What are Collaborative Sessions?

A collaborative session is a shared assistant conversation that multiple team members can access simultaneously. Instead of forwarding chat history or screenshots, you can invite teammates directly into the conversation.

**Benefits:**
- Real-time visibility into assistant work
- Team members can ask follow-up questions
- No context switching or information loss
- Automatic notifications when updates arrive
- Organized conversation history

## How Sharing Works

### Share a Session

To invite team members to a session:

1. Open an assistant session
2. In the **chat prompt**, click the **@ icon** (or type `@`)
3. A dropdown appears showing available team members
4. Select the team members you want to add
5. Choose whether to **notify the AI** or just your team members
6. Press **Enter** to add them to the session

![Sharing a Session](image-placeholder-share-session.png)

### What Happens After Sharing

- **Instant access** – Selected team members gain access immediately
- **Notifications** – They receive a notification about the shared session
- **Live updates** – They see all new messages and results in real-time
- **Session appears** – The shared session appears in their "Shared With Me" section
- **Unread badges** – They see unread counts if they're not actively viewing

## Permissions in Shared Sessions

All members in a shared session have **equal access**:

- ✅ View the full conversation history
- ✅ Ask the assistant follow-up questions
- ✅ See real-time updates and results
- ✅ Contribute ideas and feedback

## Session Types

### **Private Sessions**

Your personal assistant conversations. Use the **lock icon** in the chat prompt to keep a session private.

- Visible only to you
- No other team members can see or access
- Default for new sessions

### **Shared Sessions**

Shared sessions that team members can access. Indicated by the **share icon** in the chat prompt.

- Shared with invited team members
- Appears in their "Shared With Me" section
- They receive notifications of updates

### **Team-Visible Sessions**

Conversations your entire team can access without the need to share. Indicated by the **Multi-user Icon** in the chat prompt.

- Shared with all team members
- Appears by clicking on the team assistant and then selecting the history button on the right hand side
- Notifications will be received if the chat is open only

## Real-Time Collaboration Example

**Scenario:** Code review with an AI assistant

1. **You** start a session with your code assistant
2. **You** ask the assistant to review your function
3. **You** @mention your teammate Sarah
4. **Sarah** gets a notification and joins the session
5. **Sarah** can ask the assistant follow-up questions
6. **You** both see results instantly
7. **No back-and-forth** – everything happens in one place

## Best Practices

- **Share early** – Invite teammates before asking complex questions so they have full context
- **Keep it focused** – One session per topic/task to avoid confusion
- **Mute unnecessary notifications** – Turn off notifications for sessions you're not actively working on
- **Clean up** – Remove team members when a project is complete
