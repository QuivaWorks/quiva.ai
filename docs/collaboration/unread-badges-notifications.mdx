# Unread Badges & Smart Notifications

Unread badges and smart notifications keep you informed about updates in shared sessions without overwhelming you with alerts.

## Unread Badges

### How Badges Work

**Unread count badges** appear next to sessions showing how many new messages or results you haven't viewed yet.

![Unread Badges](image-placeholder-unread-badges.png)

**Badge indicators:**
- **1-9** – Exact count of unread items
- **9+** – More than 9 unread items
- **No badge** – Everything is read

### Where Badges Appear

- **Shared With Me section** – Shows unread counts for shared sessions
- **Group headers** – Cumulative count for all sessions in a group
- **Tab indicators** – Global task tabs show unread counts
- **Sidebar** – Shared session folders show badge aggregates

### How Badges Clear

Badges automatically clear when you:

1. **Open the session** – Viewing the conversation marks items as read
2. **Scroll to the bottom** – Reading all new messages clears the badge
3. **Close and reopen** – Coming back to a session resets the view
4. **Refresh page** – Forces a refresh of unread status

## Real-Time Notifications

### What Triggers a Notification

You'll receive notifications when:

- ✅ A team member replies in a shared session
- ✅ The AI completes a task in a shared session
- ✅ A new session is shared with you
- ✅ Someone removes you from a session (optional)

### Notification Channels

**In-app notifications:**
- Visual toast notifications in the bottom-right
- Sound alert (configurable)
- Badge updates on tabs and sidebars

**Browser notifications:**
- If enabled in your browser and notification settings
- Work even when the tab is not in focus

### Notification Settings

Control notifications in **Account Management → Notification Settings**:

- **Enable/disable** real-time notifications
- **Sound on/off** – Toggle notification audio
- **By session type** – Different settings for shared vs. private sessions
- **Quiet hours** – Set times when notifications are muted

## Smart Notification Features

### Do Not Disturb

Pause notifications temporarily:

1. Click the **bell icon** in the top navigation
2. Select **Mute for 1 hour** or **Mute until tomorrow**
3. Notifications resume automatically

### Notification Preview

Hover over a notification to see:

- **Who** – Which team member or AI took the action
- **What** – What was added (message, result, etc.)
- **When** – Timestamp of the update

### Grouping Notifications

When multiple updates happen in the same session:

- **Grouped together** – See all updates from one session in one notification
- **Reduces noise** – Instead of 5 notifications, you see 1 with "5 new updates"
- **Single action** – Click once to jump to that session

## Badge Strategies

### Strategy 1: Active Monitoring

**Use when:** You're actively collaborating on a live project

- Keep notifications enabled
- Respond to badges immediately
- Check "Shared With Me" frequently

### Strategy 2: Asynchronous Collaboration

**Use when:** Team works on different schedules

- Enable notifications but mute sound
- Check badges during your work session
- Respond when you have time to focus

### Strategy 3: Daily Review

**Use when:** You want to batch your collaboration work

- Disable real-time notifications
- Mute notifications during focus time
- Review all badges once per day

## FAQ

**Q: Why don't all sessions show badges?**  
A: Badges only appear for unread messages. Once you've viewed everything, the badge disappears.

**Q: Can I turn off badges?**  
A: No, but you can disable notifications in your notification settings. Badges will still show (silently).

**Q: Why did my badge disappear without opening the session?**  
A: If another team member viewed the message, it may be marked as read for everyone (depending on your workspace settings).

**Q: Can I see which specific messages are unread?**  
A: New messages appear with a visual indicator in the session. Scroll to see what's new.
